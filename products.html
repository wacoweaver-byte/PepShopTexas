Products

Spreadsheet-style product catalog management. SKU / Item # is now the system identity used to connect products, inventory, orders, and reports. Edit product details, active status, Hot Peptide status, Stack/Blend status, research notes, COA links, and vial label lines. Inventory, pricing, and stock thresholds are handled on the Inventory page. When adding a brand-new product, you can enter the initial quantity on hand one time so the matching inventory row is created immediately.
