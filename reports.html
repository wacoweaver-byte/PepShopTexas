Payment Methods Manager

QR upload: Use the QR Image Upload button on each method. It stores the QR image with that payment method setting. You can also paste an image URL if you prefer to upload QR images into GitHub assets.

Payment methods are optional. Enabled methods appear in the cart and order confirmation email payload.

Note: Manual payment methods keep orders Payment Pending until you verify payment and mark the order paid in Admin Orders.
