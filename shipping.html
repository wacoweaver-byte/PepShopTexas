Shipping & Returns

Effective Date: June 11, 2026

Pep Shop Texas ships from Central Texas. This page explains processing, shipping, tracking, returns, and order-issue review.

Shipping

Orders ship from Central Texas after payment has been confirmed.

If your email was entered correctly, emails will be sent during these status changes: order received, order processing, payment accepted, order packed, order shipped, and order delivered.

Typical processing time is 1–2 business days, excluding weekends and holidays.

Standard shipping will be completed through USPS throughout the United States with tracking capabilities when available.

Tracking information will be provided when available. Carrier delays, weather delays, and address issues are outside the control of Pep Shop Texas.

Returns

Due to product handling, storage, and research-product integrity concerns, returns are not accepted once an order has shipped.

If an order arrives damaged, missing an item, or incorrect, contact Pep Shop Texas within 48 hours of delivery so the issue can be reviewed.

Damaged, missing, or incorrect item concerns are reviewed case by case.

Evidence by way of photographs or videos may be required during investigation before a claim is approved for refund.

Contact

For shipping or order questions, contact Pep Shop Texas at support@pepshoptexas.com. Please include your order number when available.

Research-Use Notice

Products and information provided by Pep Shop Texas are intended for research-use context only where applicable. Customers are responsible for reviewing all notices shown during registration, checkout, and order review.
