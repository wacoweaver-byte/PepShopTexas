Research-Only FAQ

Are products intended for human or veterinary use?

No. Products are provided for research-use context only and are not for human or veterinary use.

Is this medical advice?

No. Website content is not medical advice, diagnostic guidance, treatment guidance, dosing advice, or a recommendation for human or animal use.

Does every product have a COA?

No blanket claim should be made. Batch-specific third-party testing is available only when listed for a specific product or lot.

What if a product image shows a vial or label?

Product images are representative visual references. Actual packaging, labeling, cap color, batch, lot, and documentation may vary.

Can product composition be verified?

Composition should be reviewed through batch-specific testing documentation when listed. If no lot-specific document is listed, do not assume verification.

Product Image Disclaimer

Product images, vial renderings, label designs, and package visuals are representative only. They may not reflect the final product, actual lot, actual batch, final label, cap color, or available testing documentation. Always refer to listed batch-specific documentation when available.
