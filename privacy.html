Privacy Policy

This starter privacy policy explains the basic information Pep Shop Texas may collect for account, order, support, and website operation purposes.

1. Information Collected

Pep Shop Texas may collect information you provide when registering, logging in, placing an order, contacting support, or using account features.

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number when provided
  • Shipping/contact address when provided
  • Order history and order items
  • Account profile information
  • Support messages or customer notes

2. How Information Is Used

Information may be used to operate accounts, process orders, provide order updates, respond to support requests, manage inventory/order records, prevent fraud or misuse, and improve website functionality.

3. Payment Information

Pep Shop Texas should not store sensitive payment credentials directly unless a compliant payment processor or approved payment system is implemented. Manual payment workflows may require payment confirmation and administrative review.

4. Email Communications

Email may be used for account-related messages, order confirmations, order status updates, support responses, or policy notices.

5. Data Storage

Customer account and order information may be stored using third-party website/database services used to operate the site.

6. Sharing

Information may be shared with service providers required to operate the website, process orders, send emails, maintain records, or comply with lawful obligations. Pep Shop Texas does not intend to sell customer personal information.

7. Customer Requests

Customers may contact support to request correction of account information or ask questions about stored information.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact Pep Shop Texas support using the contact page.
